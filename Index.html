Apex Predators

Orca

Orcas, also known as killer whales are the largest member of the dolphin family. They hunt sharks, seals, and fish. They are highly social, they travel in pods. Some populations are endangered.

Lion

Lions live in prides and are native to Africa and India. Males have manes, females hunt together. Lions are listed as vulnerable species.

Polar bears live in the Arctic and primarily hunt seals. They are the largest land carnivores and are classified as vulnerable.
